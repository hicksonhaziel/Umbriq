Frequently Asked Questions

Is this a public order book? expand_more
No. Umbriq operates as a private RFQ network. Orders are never broadcast to public mempools or standard DEX order books, protecting your trading intent from front-running and slippage.
Who sees my RFQ? expand_more
Only the specific, vetted institutional liquidity providers you authorize. You control the disclosure scope for every request.
How does compliance access work? expand_more
Umbriq allows you to generate read-only access keys for internal compliance teams or external auditors. They can view settlement records and execution quality without having trading privileges or publicizing the data.
Which wallets and chains are supported? expand_more
Umbriq is currently native to Solana, optimized for high-throughput institutional settlement. We support enterprise wallet solutions including Fireblocks, Copper, and standard SPL-compatible hardware wallets.
